### 🗓️ Weeks 1–4: The Foundation (Late Oct – Late Nov 2024)

The journey began with a focused effort on understanding *why* we were building LeadNow and *how* AI could meaningfully support educator development. During these first four weeks we prioritised requirements gathering, technical research, and team onboarding to align on product and engineering goals.

<!--truncate-->

Key activities in this phase included:

- Requirements gathering with stakeholders to clarify goals and success criteria.
- Technical research to evaluate RAG, LangChain, LangGraph, and suitable hosting options.
- Establishing onboarding and collaboration processes so the team could work effectively across disciplines.

This period created the blueprint for both product and engineering decisions that followed, ensuring the team shared a common vision before development started.
