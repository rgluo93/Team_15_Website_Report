Weeks 9-10: 12/01 - 25/01

Preparing for elevator pitch and development of other major features

<!--truncate-->

Our first meeting back after the holidays took place on 15/1. During the meeting, we went over the codebase for the mobile version of LeadNow and acquired access to the repository, thus starting development for the mobile app. We also learnt about the daily routine of education officers, such as planning school visits and following up on teachers' progress, so that we could tailor our features further according to this. Our team also met up to discuss our plans for the coming weeks, allocating tasks to each member to allow us to focus on our own major feature. 

During this period we also started working on retrieval-augmented generation pipelines for the LLM service. We created the tool used to chunk PDF files, which takes a directory of PDFs and extracts their text content, transforms it into vector embeddings and loads the resulting vectors into the database.
