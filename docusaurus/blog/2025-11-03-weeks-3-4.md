Weeks 3-4: 03/11 - 16/11

These two weeks continued stakeholder meetings and produced the project proposal and initial specifications to guide development.

<!--truncate-->

Our second official meeting took place on 5/11. From here on, we settled on a schedule for weekly meetings. We discussed our proposed features with the client, including a multi-language feature with Swahili, personalized feedback, and a chatbot for education officers. Afterwards, we started to fill in the project specifications and proposal template for Tech to the Rescue. The Dignitas team also shared their GitHub repository for the LeadNow web platform with us, allowing us to review and get familiarized with their codebase.
