Weeks 5-6: 17/11 - 01/12

Meeting with the lead developer of LeadNow and familiarizing with their backend

<!--truncate-->

By the start of week 5, we finalized and submitted our project proposal document. We also managed to settle on a time to meet with their lead developer, who walked us through the existing system architecture, including the backend built using Laravel, a PHP-based framework, and the MySQL database. We also received extra guidelines on the WhatsApp chatbot for education officers, a key feature that they requested.
After the meeting, we received credentials to access the database and the LeadNow devspace, signalling the beginning of backend development. We did research on tools that were best suited to implement the requested features, deciding to use a FastAPI backend, LangChain and Azure OpenAI in order to create the chat service. We then set up the above technologies and started working on the chat service.
