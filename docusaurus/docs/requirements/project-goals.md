# Project Goals


At a high level, we aim to raise the quality of Kenyan education by:

- Improving access to tailored, high-quality professional development for educators
- Automating content delivery and performance feedback for scalable coaching
- Providing targeted interventions for at-risk users to maximize learning retention
- Enhancing training methodology through data-driven insights

The online coaching platform, **LeadNow by Dignitas**, is used by school leaders, teachers, and education officers across Kenya. Our solution aims to scale and enhance the impact of LeadNow by providing teachers with transferable skills and necessary professional development, while also allowing education officers to access data-driven insights regarding teacher development. This will ultimately raise the overall teaching quality in Kenya.

We have outlined several key aspects of the LeadNow platform that can be improved:

### Accessibility

The content of LeadNow is currently entirely in English. While English and Swahili are both official languages of Kenya, Swahili is more widely spoken and preferred by the majority of educators using the platform. We aim to adopt a translation model that facilitates translation from English to Swahili, encouraging deeper understanding and more effective content delivery.

### Engagement

Although LeadNow is widely used across schools in Kenya, not all schools and teachers are adopting it fully. Our client has reported unsatisfactory engagement, measured by low module completion rates and low scores in numerous schools. To address this, we aim to provide simple, easy-to-use monitoring and analytics tools for education officers (EOs) in the form of a chatbot. This tool should be able to turn data into next-best coaching actions for EOs before, during, and after school visits, as well as monitor user engagement metrics and proactively identify individuals at risk of disengagement or dropping out, enabling timely interventions and targeted support.

### Learning Effectiveness

Currently, LeadNow is lacking in one of the most critical parts of learning: feedback. Without feedback, teachers can’t measure progress, understand mistakes, or get guidance in real time. We intend to address this by generating personalised learning recommendations for each user by analysing their activity logs, assessment results, and engagement patterns within the LeadNow platform. Additionally, we will integrate an AI-driven chatbot into the scenario-based learning module to simulate realistic classroom challenges, enabling users to practice decision-making and problem-solving in interactive conversations.
