# Requirements Gathering

## User Survey Questionnaire

After evaluating the different methods of identifying requirements, we decided to use a questionnaire because it allowed us to reach the majority of the existing user base and was the best method to quantify their general feelings about the current version of the app. We surveyed active users and gathered their feedback through multiple choice questions. ## Interviews

After gathering data from the questionnaire, we also decided to conduct semi-structured interviews to probe deeper into the specific motivations and goals of our users. The transcripts below were recorded from two different interviews, one with an education officer and one with an educator.

### From Education Officer

**How do you ensure high quality education in schools?**

I work with school leaders to ensure that teachers align with national education policies. In order to achieve this, I go to schools in person and sit in classrooms, observing the teachers' interactions and class management ability, the infrastructure and resources available to them, and the students' academic performance. I write reports which are used to plan resource allocation and teacher training.

**How does LeadNow help in the process?**

It helps me get data to inform my decision-making. I review lesson observation data and learning outcomes; then, I guide educators to set clear improvement goals and follow up on their progress remotely.

**What AI features in the LeadNow app would help you improve your productivity?**

An AI assistant that suggests personalised coaching tips or next modules to learn based on teacher profiles would save time.

### From Educator

**Does LeadNow help you better your teaching skills?**

Yes.

**How?**

I learn how to handle real life situations that are simulated in LeadNow's scenarios.

**Is the app easy to use and learn with? Does it motivate you to improve?**

The controls and navigation of the app is easy, but the content is sometimes a little boring. I struggle to keep motivation when I am just watching videos and have no support when I am confused.

**Does the expert advice in scenarios help with the confusion?**

No. The expert advice is always related to the scenario itself. It does not relate to any of my responses to scenario questions.

**Would you use an AI chatbot which guides you through learning and personalises advice for you?**

Yes, that would be helpful.
