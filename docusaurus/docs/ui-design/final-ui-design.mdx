# Final UI Design

## User Progress Summary

An AI-generated summary displayed on the teacher's dashboard, providing a personalised overview of their learning journey, progress, achievements, and recommended next steps.

![User Progress Summary — Desktop](/img/ui-design-images/final-user-summary.png)

<img src="/img/ui-design-images/final-user-summary-mobile.jpg" alt="User Progress Summary — Mobile" width="35%" />

---

## Scenario-Based Learning Feedback

After a teacher submits their response to a scenario question, an LLM compares it against the expert answer and generates personalised feedback covering strengths, areas for improvement, and actionable recommendations.

![Scenario-Based Learning Feedback — Desktop](/img/ui-design-images/final-scenario-feedback.png)

<img src="/img/ui-design-images/final-scenario-feedback-mobile.png" alt="Scenario-Based Learning Feedback — Mobile" width="35%" />

---

## English / Swahili Translation

Teachers can translate scenario content between English and Swahili with a single button, making the material accessible regardless of language preference.

![English / Swahili Translation](/img/ui-design-images/final-translate.png)

---

## AI Chatbot (Teacher)

Teachers can ask the AI Assistant questions about module content at any time. The chatbot provides contextual answers drawn from the learning material, supporting teachers when they are confused or need clarification.

<img src="/img/ui-design-images/final-chatbot.png" alt="AI Chatbot — Desktop" width="35%" />

<img src="/img/ui-design-images/final-chatbot-mobile.jpg" alt="AI Chatbot — Mobile" width="35%" />

## Education Officer AI Agent

Education officers interact with a dedicated AI Agent that can query live data, identify underperforming teachers, and take actions such as composing and sending WhatsApp reminders — all from a single chat interface.
